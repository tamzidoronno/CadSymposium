CAD Symposio

Our Vision

We want to give developers, designers and makers a chance to work on the next generation of technology and create new things and prototypes that can solve the most pressing problems of our time such as traffic, affordable healthcare, education for all etc.

Our Mission

We want to involve the leading technology companies by providing us with their problem statements and hardware toolkits such as chips, gadgets and gears which could then be used by the makers, designers and developers to craft new technologies.

Our Target

The winner will get the chance to represent Bangladesh at Makerfest 2015 held in January. http://makerfest.com/ and convert their technology into a successful startup in Bangladesh and also globally.
